About Us

Mateh Yehuda

The regional council administers moshavim, kibbutzim, Arab villages and other rural settlements in the Jerusalem corridor, north and south of the Jerusalem-Tel Aviv highway, from Jerusalem in the southeast to Latrun in the northwest, and down to the area of Beit Shemesh (Ha'ela Valley) in the south.

Who Are We

We are a group of friends that grew in a town nearby, and due to the lack of things to do in the area in the weekends, and the lack of cool places to hang out, we decided to establish the Alternative Cafe. Our truck is here to serve fresh produce to the people living in the area, and to the travelers coming to the region in the weekends.

What do we serve

Coffee

We serve fresh grounded coffee roasted by local businesses. Every week we get new fresh roasted coffee beans and grind them on the spot when you order your coffee, so how do you like your cup?

Sandwiches

Here you can get sandwiches with special breads baked daily, filled with fresh cheeses and vegetables coming from the farms of our lovely neighborhood

Burger Weekends

Every weekend we light up the charcoals and making a great burger feast, with special toppings changing every week

Beers & Wines

Our neighborhood is filled with small breweries and wineries, Accompany your food with local beverages. Every week we host another winery and brewery, so keep coming every week for new surprises!
